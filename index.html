Bria Salmena

Professional Musician and Brand Ambassador

Bria is a Toronto native who gravitated towards the lime light at an early age. She jumped at any opportunity to preform in front of anyone who was willing to listen, often with no end in sight. That passion has evolved from a childhood expression to a fulfilling career. Her career in the music industry spans over a decade, most notably as the right hand woman in Country Star Orville Peck's band. Not only does Bria play guitar, keys and sing background vocals,fans know her best for her signature makeup look/style.Bria does not identify creatively within any one musical genre. Her taste and skill is reflected in everything from Country to Punk. She is determined to break down barriers in order to champion self acceptance and give space for everyone's inner freak. Bria wants to align herself is interested in working with other alternative creators in fashion,music and beauty.
